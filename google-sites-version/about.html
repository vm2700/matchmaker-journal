About Kismet

Our mission, vision, and the team behind the magic

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Mala Mehta after her own journey through the world of online dating, Kismet was born from the belief that meaningful connections shouldn't be left to chance.

After years of research in relationship psychology and compatibility factors, we created a platform that goes beyond surface-level matching to help people find partners who truly complement them on a deeper level.

Today, Kismet has helped thousands of couples find lasting relationships based on genuine compatibility and shared values.

Our Mission & Values

Authentic Connections

We believe in fostering genuine relationships based on compatibility, shared values, and mutual respect.

Inclusive Matchmaking

We're committed to creating a platform where everyone feels welcome, represented, and valued.

Privacy & Safety

We prioritize user safety and data privacy in every aspect of our service.

Continuous Improvement

We're always refining our algorithms and approach based on relationship research and user feedback.
